Beautiful ocean breeze

Greek islands like Crete, Mykonos, Naxos, Milos, Paros, Ios, Lefkada, Zakynthos, Rhodes, Karpathos, and more have some of the nicest beaches. The best are Agios Nikitas in Lefkada and Agios Georgios in Naxos for families with children. Paradise Beach in Mykonos and Pounda Beach in Paros are the ideal places to party.

Ancient greek

The Acropolis of Athens and its monuments are universal symbols of the classical spirit and civilization and form the greatest architectural and artistic complex bequeathed by Greek Antiquity to the world.

Large soft bread rings coated in sesame seeds, known as koulouri, are a common breakfast food for locals traveling to work in any of the major Greek towns, including Athens or Thessaloniki. They are frequently consumed while on the go with a cup of coffee and served from yellow street carts.
